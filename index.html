Our craft

Every pair is cut, stitched and finished by hand in our Karachi workshop.

We work with full-grain and top-grain hides sourced locally, shaped over traditional lasts, and saddle-stitched for a seam that flexes instead of cracking. No shortcuts, no synthetic fillers — just leather, thread, and time.
